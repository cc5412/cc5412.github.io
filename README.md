# cc5412.github.io
Welcome to the portfolio of Christian Carvajal!

Christian is an MSUP candidate at Columbia GSAPP. He holds a BA in Organismic &amp; Evolutionary Biology from Harvard University. Between his studies, he worked in documentary film and nonfiction media for 10 years. Topics ranged from sharks to NFTs.

## Projects

### [GIS (2025), Suitable Sites for City-owned Grocery Stores](https://github.com/cc5412/GIS_Final-Project)
An analysis of food insecurity and grocery affordability in order to ground policy around city-owned grocery stores to the daily lives of the most vulnerable New Yorkers. Used **ArcGIS Pro** and **Python**.

### [Spatial-Temporal Data Mining (2026), Spatio-Temporal Analysis of Water Temperatures in Long Island Sound: Comparing ARIMA and STARIMA Models]
